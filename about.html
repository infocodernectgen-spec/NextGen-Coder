Our Story

Founded in 2010, Gyan Bakery started with a simple mission: to bring the authentic taste of home-baked goods to everyone. What began as a small home kitchen has now grown into a beloved community bakery.

We believe in using only the finest ingredients - fresh cream, pure butter, and locally sourced fruits. No preservatives, just pure love.
